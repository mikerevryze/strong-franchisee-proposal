========================
CORE CONCEPT (DO NOT BREAK)
========================

Each studio is its own investment.

- Franchisee pays:
  - Revryze fee (per studio)
  - Ad spend (per studio)

- Franchisor (Alloy corporate):
  - Pays nothing
  - Earns 7% royalty on all revenue generated

The calculator must clearly show:
1) Per-studio ROI (using fee + ad spend as investment)
2) Franchisor royalty upside (pure upside, separate)

DO NOT show:
- Aggregate franchisee spend across all studios
- “How much Revryze makes”
- Corporate “net investment” or negative numbers

========================
STUDIO-LEVEL ROI (PRIMARY)
========================

Inputs (keep existing sliders):
- Revryze fee per studio:
  - $40,000 standard
  - $30,000 if 10+ studio commitment
- Monthly ad spend (studio-paid)
- Members acquired per studio
- Monthly member value ($350 default)
- Member lifetime in months (12 default)

Calculations:
- LTV per member = monthly_value × lifetime_months
- Revenue per studio = members × LTV
- Total studio investment = Revryze fee + ad spend
- ROI multiple = revenue_per_studio ÷ total_studio_investment

Display clearly:
- Revenue generated (per studio)
- Total investment (per studio)
- ROI multiple (per studio)

This is the main proof.

========================
GUARANTEE & REFUND LOGIC
========================

Guarantee:
- 100 members per studio

Refund logic (prorated):
refund_percentage = (100 − members_acquired) ÷ 100
refund_amount = refund_percentage × Revryze fee

Rules:
- Refund only adjusts Revryze fee
- Refund does NOT change:
  - Revenue
  - ROI numerator
  - Franchisor royalty

ROI should update dynamically after refund.
